Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Lilurls respects your privacy. Lilurls does not collect personal data from its users. This document details our practices regarding any non-personal data we may gather and our commitment to securing your digital privacy.

2. Information Collected

No Personal Data Collection

At Lilurls, we are committed to protecting your privacy. In line with this commitment, we want to clarify our stance on personal data collection:

  • No Personal Information: Lilurls does not collect any personal information from its users. When you use our service to shorten URLs, you are not required to provide any information that could be used to personally identify you, such as your name, address, email address, or phone number.
  • Usage Data: While we do not collect personal information, we may collect non-identifiable usage data. This includes information such as the number of times a shortened URL is accessed, which is used for the sole purpose of monitoring service performance and enhancing user experience.
  • Cookies: We do not use cookies to track personal information. If cookies are used, they are solely for the purpose of improving user interaction with our site and the sites security, and no personal data is recorded.
  • Third-Party Services: We do not sell or share any data with third parties, except as required by law.

3. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the data we collect from loss, theft, or unauthorized use. However, no internet-based service can guarantee complete security. We encourage users to safeguard their own personal information online.

4. Updating Information

Users can request updates or corrections to their information by contacting us. We will respond to these requests in a timely manner and are committed to maintaining accurate data.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to access, correct, delete, or transfer any data they provide. Please contact us to exercise these rights. We are committed to supporting our users' rights to their data.

6. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our website's contact page. We aim to address all inquiries promptly and thoroughly.

7. Third-Party Service Providers

Lilurls uses third-party services to enhance your experience and our service's functionality. This includes Google AdSense for displaying ads and Google reCAPTCHA v3 for protecting our site against spam and abuse.

Please be aware that while Lilurls does not collect personal data, these services may collect user data. Google AdSense may collect data for the purpose of displaying personalized advertisements, and Google reCAPTCHA v3 may collect data to determine whether the visitor is a human or a bot.

Your interaction with Google AdSense and reCAPTCHA may result in data collection managed by Google. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy and ad settings for more information and control over your data.

By using Lilurls, you acknowledge and consent to the data collection and use practices of these third-party services as outlined in Google's privacy policy.

8. Google's Use of Data

In connection with our use of Google services such as Google AdSense and Google reCAPTCHA, please note that Google may place and read cookies on your browser, or use web beacons to collect information as a result of ad serving on our website. This helps in providing personalized ads and analyzing website traffic.

For detailed information on how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services, please visit Google's official policy page on how they use data from partner sites: Google's policy on partner sites.

By using Lilurls, you consent to the use of cookies and data collection as per the practices described in Google's policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Lilurls is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age. If we become aware of such collection, we will take appropriate steps to delete this information.

10. Cookies and Site Security

  • Security-Enhancing Cookies: At Lilurls, our use of cookies is strictly limited to enhancing the security of our site. We utilize cookies to manage CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery) tokens, which are an essential part of protecting our service and its users.
  • Mandatory for Service Use: These security cookies are not optional; they are a necessary component for the safe operation of Lilurls. By using our service, you consent to the use of these cookies as they are integral to providing you with secure access to our features.
  • No Personal Data Stored: It's important to note that these cookies do not store any personal information. Their sole purpose is to maintain the security and integrity of user sessions on our site, safeguarding against unauthorized requests and activities.
  • Commitment to Privacy: We remain committed to respecting your privacy. The limited use of cookies for security purposes aligns with our dedication to ensuring a secure and private experience for all our users.

12. Privacy Policy Updates

Lilurls reserves the right to update or change this policy at any time, the most current version will always be available on this page.
